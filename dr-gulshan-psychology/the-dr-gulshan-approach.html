Not Therapy as Usual

Therapy isn't just about symptom relief—it's about becoming whole. I offer a unique approach for adults who are ready to stop performing wellness and start actually healing.

Whether you're carrying the unspoken weight of your history or feel like your past is still living in your bones, this work is designed to meet you with depth, dignity, and care.

Treatment Modalities

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Gaining awareness of unhelpful thought patterns and learning how to shift them in practical, structured ways.

Cognitive Processing Therapy (CPT)

A specialized form of CBT for trauma—focused on identifying and transforming the beliefs trauma leaves behind, especially around safety, power, and worthiness.

Internal Family Systems (IFS)

Helping you explore the different "parts" of your inner world—like the high achiever or the people pleaser—and building compassion for each of them.

Positive Psychology & Mindfulness

Rebuilding meaning, resilience, and creativity while using grounding practices to tune into the present moment without judgment.

A Trauma Therapy Method Like No Other

I also offer Haunted House Therapy™—a trauma-repair method I've developed for adults navigating deep, layered pain. We don't just talk about the past—we walk through it. Room by room.
