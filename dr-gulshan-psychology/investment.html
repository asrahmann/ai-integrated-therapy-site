Do I Accept Insurance?

I am an out-of-network provider, which means I do not bill insurance directly. However, I can provide a "superbill" (a special receipt) that you can submit to your insurance provider for possible reimbursement.

Working outside of insurance allows me to provide fully personalized, confidential care without being limited by diagnosis requirements, treatment caps, or external clinical reviews.

Disclaimer: This practice is not equipped to respond to emergencies. If in crisis, please dial 911.
